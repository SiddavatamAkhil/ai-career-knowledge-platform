Aurora Robotics Employee Handbook

1. Company Overview
Aurora Robotics was founded in 2019 and builds autonomous warehouse robots
for mid-size logistics companies. The company is headquartered in Austin,
Texas, and has satellite engineering offices in Berlin and Bangalore.

2. Leave Policy
Full-time employees accrue 18 days of paid time off (PTO) per year, plus
10 company holidays. PTO accrues monthly and can be carried over up to a
maximum of 10 unused days into the following calendar year. Parental leave
is 16 weeks fully paid for the primary caregiver and 8 weeks for the
secondary caregiver.

3. Remote Work Policy
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week. Fully remote arrangements
require director-level approval and are reviewed every 6 months. All remote
employees must be available during core hours of 11am-4pm in their local
time zone for team synchronization.

4. Expense Reimbursement
Employees can expense up to $75/month for home internet, and up to $50 per
meal for approved business travel. Expense reports must be submitted within
30 days of the purchase date through the internal Concur portal.

5. Performance Review Cycle
Aurora Robotics runs performance reviews twice a year, in April and October.
Reviews include a self-assessment, manager assessment, and peer feedback
from at least two colleagues. Promotion decisions are made following the
October review cycle.

6. Security Policy
All employees must enable two-factor authentication on their company
accounts within their first week. Laptops are encrypted by default using
company-managed FileVault/BitLocker policies. Any lost or stolen device
must be reported to IT within 2 hours of discovery.
